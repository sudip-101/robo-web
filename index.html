ABOUT US

The Official Robotics Club of KGEC is founded to establish the connection between Innovations and Tech. skills in the field of ROBOTICS among the engineering students.

OUR MOTTO

Think, Build, Renovate.

OUR GOAL

The KGEC - Robotics Society is aimed at creating a healthy robotics culture inside the college premises, where students apart from their academic curriculum and chores get to learn something which is related to the live industry work culture, gain knowledge on core related domains, get hands-on experience and guidance by their seniors themselves who are actively contributing and building stuffs in the field itself. Developing a building and innovating environment through workshops and projects is what this club strives for.

OUR VISION

The Vision is to make our college KGEC reach heights where we will be competing and winning national events, the events held in IITs, NITs, national level robotics competitions and championships, we sought to prepare a group of individuals who will be technically sound and prepared and can represent the college at Robotics Events and Competitions held at the National Level all over India.
